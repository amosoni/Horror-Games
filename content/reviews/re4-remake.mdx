Resident Evil 4 Remake modernizes the 2005 classic with updated graphics, improved controls, and smart quality-of-life tweaks.

The pacing remains top tier, and the atmosphere is dense and thrilling. While some sections are streamlined, the core experience is intact and better balanced.

Pros:

- Tight gunplay and great encounter design
- Superb atmosphere and audio
- Smart modernization without losing soul

Cons:

- Minor pacing dips in late chapters
- Occasional difficulty spikes
